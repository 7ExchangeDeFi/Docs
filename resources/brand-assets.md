# Brand Assets

Official 7.Exchange brand assets for press, partners, and community use.

## Brand name

The correct brand name is **7.Exchange** (with the period). In contexts where a period causes formatting issues, use **7Exchange** as a fallback. Do not use "Seven Exchange" in external materials unless required for legal documents.

## Logo usage

- Use the official logo files provided below. Do not recreate, modify, or distort the logo.
- Maintain clear space around the logo do not crowd it with other elements.
- Do not change the logo colors. Use the provided color variations for light and dark backgrounds.
