# Limit Orders

{% hint style="info" %}
Limit Orders are under development. This page describes the planned feature. Follow our updates on [X](https://x.com/7exchangeDeFi) and [Discord](https://discord.gg/your-server) for launch announcements.
{% endhint %}

Limit orders will allow you to set a target price for a swap and have it execute automatically when the market reaches your level.

## How it will work

1. Select your source and destination tokens.
2. Enter the amount you want to swap.
3. Set your **target price** — the rate at which you want the swap to execute.
4. Submit the order.
5. When the market price reaches your target, the swap executes automatically.

You won't need to monitor the market or be online when the order fills.

## Key properties

- **No continuous signing** — Once the order is placed, execution happens without requiring additional wallet signatures.
- **Cross-chain support** — Limit orders will work across chains, not just within a single network.
- **Authenticated execution** — Orders use the same quote authentication and execution integrity pipeline as regular swaps.

## Use cases

- Buying a token when it dips to a specific price
- Selling into strength at a target level
- Automating trades without watching the market
