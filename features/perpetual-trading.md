# Perpetual Trading

{% hint style="info" %}
Perpetual Trading is under development. This page describes the planned feature. Follow our updates on [X](https://x.com/7exchangeDeFi) and [Discord](https://discord.gg/your-server) for launch announcements.
{% endhint %}

7.Exchange will aggregate perpetual trading across multiple on-chain venues, bringing the same routing and best-execution approach to leveraged trading.

## How it will work

- Open, manage, and close perpetual positions from the 7.Exchange interface.
- The routing engine will compare available venues and route to the one offering the best execution for your position.
- Trade perps across chains without manually switching networks or managing multiple interfaces.

## What to expect

- Unified position management across multiple perp DEXs
- Cross-chain margin routing
- Integrated trade history alongside your swap activity
